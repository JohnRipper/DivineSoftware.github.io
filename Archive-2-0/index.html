The Archive 2.0

First of all, welcome, you have overcame the internet waves and arrived to us.
If you don't know yet, this is a place for resources, knowledge and interesting topics.

Can I join?

Are you an artist? Computer hacker? Engineer? Philosopher? Doesn't matter at all. Just have something interesting to talk about. (And a skill to make a socket client hehe)

What is this hackerspace about?

This place is meant to be a well of knowledge. There will be always someone better than you and because of that I made this place suited for gaining knowledge and helping people.

Why are you not on a hackerspaces map?

Hackerspace is defined as a physical place with all the electronics stuff, but I believe it can be a chat room or even a blog, because everything stands on members.
